IRB Decision Guide

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is IRB approval? - IRB approval is a review of your research plan to ensure ethical standards are met.
  • When is IRB review required? - IRB review is required when your research involves human subjects or identifiable private information.
  • How do I know if my research needs IRB review? - Use the decision tree on the Decision Points page to assess whether your study requires IRB review.
  • What forms do I need to submit? - Refer to the Resources page for links to the necessary forms and templates based on your research type.
  • How do I modify an existing IRB approval? - Visit the Modifications section under the Decision Points page to learn more about the steps for submitting modifications.
